Liquor Ammonia

Liquor Ammonia is a combination of anhydrous ammonia and water. This chemical solution is applicable in the manufacturing of products in multiple industries, including, plastics, textiles, sanitizer, chemical fertilizer, and as a cleaning agent. This solution is used to clean household things like electronic appliances, and mirrors. The growing agriculture sector is gaining traction for the use of chemical fertilizers. This boosts the use of Liquor Ammonia. Being the main ingredient in the production of the sanitizer this makes an ideal choice for the manufacturer. Additionally, it is used in household appliances as well, like, in removing stains and filling the gas in the refrigerator.
